## 11. Legal Contract Protections (Must-Haves)

Before signing any listing agreement, ensure it includes:

| Protection | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Jurisdiction** | Neutral jurisdiction (Hong Kong, Singapore preferred). Avoid unfamiliar jurisdictions. |
| **Clear payment terms** | Exact amount, due date, payment method (USDT on specific chain), corporate wallet address |
| **Listing timeline** | Defined "go-live" date. If they fail to list within 90 days of payment, right to full refund |
| **Delisting notice** | Minimum 30-day notice period + clear, objective reasons for delisting |
| **No exclusivity** | Contract must NOT prevent listing on other exchanges |
| **IP and branding** | No ownership rights to Quillon brand, logo, or intellectual property |
| **Refund clause** | Full or partial refund if listing never goes live or is terminated within X days |
| **Token custody** | Clear terms on how HIBT handles QUG tokens allocated for activities — are unused tokens returned? |
