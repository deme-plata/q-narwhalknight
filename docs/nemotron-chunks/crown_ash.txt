TOPIC: Crown & Ash — Blockchain Strategy Game

## What is Crown & Ash?
Crown & Ash is a **blockchain-native strategy game** built on Quillon Graph.
Players compete for territory, form alliances, build civilizations, and battle for dominance.
All game state lives on-chain — every action is a blockchain transaction.

## Gameplay
- **Territory control**: Claim and defend hexagonal territory tiles
- **Alliances**: Form political alliances with other players
- **Battles**: Challenge opponents for territory in real-time combat
- **Economy**: Mine resources, trade with other players, build infrastructure
- **Succession**: When a ruler falls, succession events trigger new power dynamics

## AI-Generated Narratives
Crown & Ash uses AI to generate dynamic narratives:
- Battle descriptions, succession speeches, diplomatic events
- Powered by local AI inference (Nemotron/Mistral models)
- Each game event produces unique, lore-rich text
- The narrative adapts to player actions and game history

## How to Play
1. Open the Quillon wallet
2. Navigate to the Crown & Ash tab
3. Create or join a game session
4. Start with a small territory and build from there
5. QUG is used for in-game transactions

## On-Chain Gaming
Every game action is a Quillon transaction:
- Territory claims → smart contract calls
- Battles → resolved by on-chain random oracle
- Resource transfers → QUG or game token transfers
- All outcomes are transparent and verifiable
