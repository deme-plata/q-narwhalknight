TOPIC: QUG Tokenomics and Economics

## Supply
- **Maximum supply**: 21,000,000 QUG (21 million, same cap as Bitcoin)
- **Decimals**: 24 (extreme precision for micropayments and DeFi)
- **Smallest unit**: 1 × 10^-24 QUG

## Emission Schedule
- **Era 0** (current): ~2,625,000 QUG per year
- **Halving**: Every 4 years, emission cuts in half (like Bitcoin)
- Era 1: ~1,312,500 QUG/year
- Era 2: ~656,250 QUG/year
- And so on until max supply is reached

## Block Rewards
- Each block produces a mining reward
- Block time: ~1 second
- Reward per block ≈ annual emission / seconds per year
- 1% dev fee → founder wallet (funds development)
- 99% → miners (proportional to hashrate contribution)

## Transaction Fees
- Simple transfer: ~0.00002 QUG (extremely cheap)
- Token transfer: ~0.00004 QUG
- DEX swap: ~0.00006 QUG (plus 0.3% pool fee)
- Contract call: ~0.00010 QUG
- Fees are burned or paid to miners depending on network rules

## How to Get QUG
1. **Mine it**: Download q-miner and start mining (free, just needs computing power)
2. **Receive it**: Someone sends QUG to your wallet address
3. **Trade for it**: Use the DEX to swap tokens for QUG
4. **Faucet**: Small amounts available for testing (when active)

## Value Proposition
- Fixed supply (deflationary like Bitcoin)
- Post-quantum security (future-proof against quantum computers)
- Fast block times (1 second vs Bitcoin's 10 minutes)
- Low fees (fractions of a cent)
- Built-in DEX and smart contracts
- AI-powered features (chat, narrative generation)
