TOPIC: Quillon Wallet

## Creating a Wallet
1. Open the Quillon wallet app (web: quillon.xyz, or download desktop/Slint wallet)
2. Click "Create Wallet"
3. Your wallet generates an Ed25519 keypair + 12/24-word mnemonic phrase
4. **SAVE YOUR MNEMONIC PHRASE** — write it down on paper, store in a safe place
5. Your wallet address starts with `qnk` followed by 64 hex characters (67 total)

## Wallet Features
- **Send QUG**: Enter recipient address, amount, and click Send. Fee: ~0.00002 QUG
- **Receive QUG**: Share your qnk-address with the sender
- **Mining**: Built-in CPU and GPU miner (Mining tab)
- **DEX Trading**: Swap QUG for tokens directly in the wallet
- **Token Factory**: Deploy your own QRC-20 token
- **Block Explorer**: View blocks, transactions, and network stats
- **AI Chat**: Ask questions about Quillon (bottom-right corner)

## Restoring a Wallet
1. Click "Restore Wallet" or "Import Mnemonic"
2. Enter your 12/24-word mnemonic phrase
3. Your wallet and balance will be restored

## Wallet Security
- Your private key NEVER leaves your device
- Mnemonic phrase = full access to your funds. Keep it secret.
- No KYC, no registration, no email required
- Optional Tor support for full anonymity

## Desktop Wallets
- **Web wallet**: quillon.xyz (runs in browser)
- **Slint wallet**: Native desktop app (faster, GPU mining support)
  - Download: `wget https://quillon.xyz/downloads/slint-wallet-linux-x86_64`
  - Auto-updates when new version is available

## Transaction Types
- **Transfer**: Send QUG to another address
- **Token transfer**: Send QRC-20 tokens
- **Swap**: DEX trade via AMM pools
- **Contract call**: Interact with smart contracts
- **Token deployment**: Create new tokens
