TOPIC: Quillon DEX (Decentralized Exchange)

## What is the Quillon DEX?
A built-in AMM (Automated Market Maker) decentralized exchange, similar to Uniswap.
Users can trade QUG for tokens and vice versa without intermediaries.
All trades execute on-chain with transparent pricing.

## How to Trade
1. Go to the DEX tab in your wallet
2. Select the token pair (e.g., QUG/MYTOKEN)
3. Enter the amount you want to swap
4. Review the price and slippage
5. Click Swap — transaction executes instantly

## Liquidity Pools
- Pools hold reserves of two tokens (e.g., QUG + MYTOKEN)
- Price is determined by the constant product formula: x * y = k
- Anyone can add liquidity and earn trading fees
- To add liquidity: Go to Pool tab → Add Liquidity → deposit equal value of both tokens
- LP (Liquidity Provider) tokens represent your share of the pool

## Creating a Token
1. Go to Token Factory in the DEX section
2. Set token name, symbol, decimals, and initial supply
3. Pay a small QUG fee for deployment
4. Your token is live on the Quillon blockchain!
5. Create a liquidity pool: pair your token with QUG

## Token Standard: QRC-20
- Similar to Ethereum's ERC-20
- Supports: transfer, approve, transferFrom, balanceOf, totalSupply
- Deployed via WASM smart contract on Quillon's VM
- All amounts use the token's own decimal precision

## Fees
- Swap fee: 0.3% (goes to liquidity providers)
- Token deployment: Small QUG gas fee
- Transaction fee: ~0.00002 QUG per swap

## Price Discovery
- Prices are set by supply and demand in the pools
- Large trades cause price impact (slippage)
- Arbitrageurs keep prices aligned with external markets
