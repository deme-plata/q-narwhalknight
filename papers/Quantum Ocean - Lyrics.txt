════════════════════════════════════════
PRODUCTION NOTES
════════════════════════════════════════

Instruments:
  - Analog synth pad (Juno-style) for ocean ambience
  - 808 sub-bass for the whale sections
  - Arpeggiated lead synth for jellyfish bioluminescence
  - Vocoder on all spoken/whispered parts
  - Sonar ping samples (C4, F4, A4) on the bridge
  - Granular synthesis ocean waves throughout
  - Distorted electric guitar stabs on chorus drops

Sound design:
  - Each species gets its own sonic identity:
    * Jellyfish = shimmering pad + glass harmonics
    * Dolphin = pitch-bent staccato plucks
    * Octopus = detuned bass growl + ink splatter FX
    * Whale = massive sub-bass swell + foghorn
    * Seahorse = triple-delay (3 ghost echoes = superposition)
    * Nano swarm = granular particle noise
    * Fish school = rapid-fire arp (12 voices)
    * Cyber Cetus = industrial sonar + metallic reverb

Mix reference: Kavinsky meets Vangelis meets Deadmau5
