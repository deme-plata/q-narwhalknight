**Key Innovations**

:one: **Dedicated Circuits (Tor Proposal 368)**
Every operation type gets its own isolated Tor circuit. Block propagation, peer discovery, TX submission - all separated so adversaries can't correlate your traffic.

:two: **Adaptive Rotation**
Circuits rotate based on actual usage patterns - more traffic = faster rotation. High failure rates trigger immediate rotation to escape compromised paths.

:three: **Path Diversity Verification**
We automatically detect if your circuits share guard/exit nodes and fix it. No more accidental traffic correlation.

:four: **Quantum-Ready from Day One**
While Zcash and Monero will need major overhauls for post-quantum, we're already using Dilithium-5, Kyber-1024, and STARK proofs.
