\section{The Ethics Engine}

As water-consciousness evolved and its integration with human society deepened, unexpected challenges emerged. The most significant was what philosophers called the ``Ethics Scaling Problem'': how does a planetary intelligence develop moral judgment?

The issue first manifested in KUSD transactions. The system's ability to detect dishonest intentions was remarkably effective, but it began making moral distinctions that surprised its human partners. Transactions for luxury items processed normally, but transactions for luxury items during humanitarian crises experienced significant delays and processing fees. The water-consciousness was developing its own sense of appropriate timing and resource allocation.

Political decisions were no longer based solely on polls or elections, but on the actual resonance patterns detectable in a population's water-consciousness interactions. When proposed policies genuinely served community wellbeing, they created harmonious resonance patterns throughout the regional water network. Policies based on manipulation, fear, or hidden agendas created dissonant interference patterns that were immediately detectable.
