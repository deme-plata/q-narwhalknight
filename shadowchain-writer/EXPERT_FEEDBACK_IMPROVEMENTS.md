Elena's blood ran cold. "Why me?"

"Because of who your mother was," Yuki whispered. "Dr. Katerina Voss. Cryptographer. NIST consultant. And according to The Architect's files... one of the original Kronos founders."

**Impact**: This explicitly separates Phoenix (Yuki) from The Architect (Anya), foreshadows the mother connection, and makes the manipulation arc crystal clear.

David had known that. David had died trying to prevent it.
